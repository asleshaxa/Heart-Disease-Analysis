Performance Testing – Amount of Data to DB

The dataset used for the Heart Disease Analysis project contains health and lifestyle related attributes such as BMI, Smoking, Alcohol Drinking, Physical Activity, Stroke, Diabetes, Age Category and others.

After performing SQL queries and cleaning operations, the processed dataset was imported into Tableau for visualization.

Approximate Dataset Details:
- Total rows: ~4500 records
- Columns: Health indicators such as BMI, Smoking, Stroke, Diabetes, Heart Disease, etc.

Since the dataset size is moderate, the data loads quickly into Tableau and dashboards render without noticeable delay. The optimized dataset ensures smooth visualization performance.
